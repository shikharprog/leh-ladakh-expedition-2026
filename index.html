Day
01
🛣️
Chandigarh → Srinagar
📍 550 km 🏨 Night in Srinagar
The longest stretch — NH44 through Jalandhar, Pathankot, Banihal Tunnel. Early start recommended. Dal Lake awaits at journey's end.
Day
02
⛰️
Srinagar → Leh
📍 417 km 🏔 Zoji La Pass 🏨 Night in Leh
The legendary NH1 — through Sonamarg, Zoji La (11,575 ft), Drass, Kargil, Lamayuru. One of the most dramatic day-rides on Earth.
Day
03
🌅
Leh → Nubra Valley
📍 317 km 🏔 Khardung La ⚔ Visit Siachen Base 🏨 Diskit
Conquer Khardung La (17,582 ft) — world-famous high altitude pass. Descend into the surreal sand dunes of Nubra. Visit Siachen Glacier viewpoint.
Day
04
💙
Diskit → Pangong Tso (Merak Village)
📍 181 km 🌊 Pangong Tso 🏕 Stay at Merak
Pangong Tso — the magical shifting blue lake at 14,270 ft. Stay in the quieter Merak village away from the tourist throngs. Stars will blow your mind.
Day
05
🌄
Pangong Tso (Merak Village) → Hanle via Chushul
📍 142 km 🏔 Chushul Valley 🏨 Night in Hanle
Remote Chushul — the frontier village near LAC. Incredible desolate plateau riding. Hanle is home to one of the world's highest observatories.
Day
06
🔺
Umling La Summit Day
📍 200 km 🔺 Umling La — 19,024 ft ⛰ Photi La 🏨 Back to Hanle
🏆 THE CROWN JEWEL — World's highest motorable road. Hanle → Photi La → Umling La and return. Ensure bikes are in top condition. Carry warm gear, oxygen, and enough fuel. An achievement of a lifetime.
Day

Day
08
🏞️
Tso Moriri → Jispa
📍 250 km 🏔 Baralacha La 🏨 Night at Jispa
Departing Ladakh across the Baralacha La (16,040 ft). Jispa sits in the lush Bhaga Valley — a beautiful basecamp before the final descent to the plains.
Day
09
🏔️
Jispa → Manali
📍 92 km to Manali 📍 357 km to Chandigarh
Rohtang Pass descent into Manali (if open) — or push straight to Chandigarh via Kullu valley. Decide based on fatigue and timeline.
Day
10
🏁
Chandigarh → Dehradun
📍 170 km 🏁 Journey Complete
The final victory lap home. Time to decompress, relive every pass, every sunset, every cold dawn — and start planning the next one.
